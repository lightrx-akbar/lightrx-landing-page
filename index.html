About Us

LightRx acquires and operates community and digital pharmacies, combining industry-standard pharmacy management software with proprietary operational systems that streamline workflows, optimize inventory, and enhance overall performance. This approach allows us to transform each acquired pharmacy into a high-performing, patient-focused business. Our mission is to create a network of pharmacies that are both profitable and community-driven, enabling pharmacists to focus on patient care while we manage operational, regulatory, and technological challenges. With a scalable approach and commitment to innovation, LightRx is building the future of pharmacy management in the UK.
